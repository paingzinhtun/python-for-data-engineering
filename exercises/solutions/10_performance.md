# Module 10 Solutions

## Beginner
A list stores all values immediately. A generator produces values lazily as needed.

## Intermediate
Yield one row at a time and process in loops or chunks instead of building a giant list.

## Applied
Pure Python is fine for modest volumes and control-heavy logic, but very large workloads may need chunking, better storage patterns, or distributed tools.

## Scenario
Use streaming reads, chunked writes, checkpointing, and possibly a more scalable platform if runtime and volume keep growing.
